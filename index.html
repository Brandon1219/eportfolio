About Me

My name is HungWei Lin. I am from Taiwan and now I am living in Latvia. I am currently working as a product specialist in a Fintech company. I have a business background. After I stepped into my career as an analyst, I found my interest growing in programing. Then I was studying coding from online resources for years and finally got promoted to a specialist, a role that is more relevant to my goal. Since then, I have furthered knowledge from my technical issue-solving, client-facing job. I do not have an official degree in this field and I know this is what I miss. Therefore I am looking forward to this program in Computer Science. Combine with my practical experience from my work, I hope I can broaden my knowledge and bring my career to the next goal.
